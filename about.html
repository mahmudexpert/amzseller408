About

Our core values and high standards are readily apparent with each personal interaction and business solution we provide. They guide our priorities and distinguish us from the competition by setting standards of excellence in a process that aligns the best level of proficiency and expertise with our client’s exacting specifications.

EXPECT THE BEST - WE DO

Integrity and commitment drive us to provide a solution that exceeds expectations. This dedication is inherent in everything we do, in every step we take. Our reputation for excellence is the reason we are trusted as a highly responsive and diligent partner that always follows through on every commitment we make to our clients. Only by following a continuing dedication to excellence can we expect to achieve the best results.

OUR OPEN AND HONEST APPROACH

Sharing knowledge and operating in an open manner establishes a strong and lasting relationship. IRIS works with our clients to gain a deep understanding of their business protocols, culture and integral staffing objectives. We seek to offer relevant information about each sourcing solution we provide, coupled with valuable IT market data that enables them to become more informed regarding their IT staffing decisions. This type of transparent enterprise collaboration produces a more comprehensive and complete business solution.

QUALIFIED RESULTS

IRIS believes that the best way to provide the right resource to our clients, along with the best opportunity for our consultants, is by strictly adhering to a multi-tiered qualification process. The reason we’ve been so successful for so many years is our devotion to a simple formula: stringently qualify each candidate and carefully analyze our client’s opportunities and culture to ensure successful results for every match.
